## Packages
leaflet | Maps visualization engine
react-leaflet | React wrapper for Leaflet
@types/leaflet | TypeScript definitions for Leaflet
framer-motion | Smooth entry animations and micro-interactions
lucide-react | Beautiful consistent icons

## Notes
Dynamic map styles require `leaflet/dist/leaflet.css` imported in index.css.
WebSocket connects to `ws://${window.location.host}/`.
The app uses a dark, sleek modern aesthetic with glassmorphism overlays on the map.
